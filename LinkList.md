## 2.驱动类

### Lilu和它的插件

| 驱动名称             | 链接地址                                     | 主要用途                                     |
| ---------------- | ---------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Lilu  | [链接](https://github.com/acidanthera/Lilu)  | 驱动注入框架，AppleALC, WhateverGreen, VirtualSMC等驱动的依赖  |
| AppleALC  | [链接](https://github.com/acidanthera/AppleALC)  | 声卡注入框架  |
